About Life Makers

Objectives

Life Makers is a nonprofit organization which works on various fields of development in Egypt.
Our major objective is giving the youth the oppotunity to participte in their homeland's development
and the experience that benefit them to be the leaders of the future.
The number of charities that cooperate with us is about 38 -in 27 governorates- and the number is 
increasing.

Vision

 comprehensive community development

Our mission

Building generations understand and believe in the concept of development in thought and act.
