Our Projects

Education is Power

The idea started in September 2011 through an initiative launched by the Vodafone Foundation for Community Development.
our dream is the Eradication of illiteracy by the efforts of the egyptian youth and the government
Up till now we achieved: 

   1- The graduation of 120 thousand person capable of reading and writing 
   2- Using the efforts of 35 volunteers
   3- Establishment of 30 youth camp for 25 thousand volunteers
   4- Preparation courses for adult literacy

Building a Life

The idea was launched in September 2012 to improve the villages, which are below the poverty line on two levels:
The first goal in the economic and infrastructure development of villages through the construction of the bishop and the delivery
of water and electricity.
The second goal in social life through a number of activities which raise awareness of the most important for children to the 
principles of public morality, and opening literacy classes and organizing medical convoys to these villages.

Up till now we achieved:
   At the economic level: we build 200 house,connecting electricity to another 50 and delivering water to 100 families.
   At the social level  : launching 20 awareness convoys,100 medical convoys and repayment the dept of 38 families.
